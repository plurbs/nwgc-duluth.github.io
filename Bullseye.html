NWGC Bulls Eye shooting

 

Beginning December 16, 2021 and December 30, 2021 practice then running every Thursday night from January 6, 2022 thru March 24, 2022 there will be Bullseye Shooting at NWGC indoor range.
December 16 and 30 will be open shooting. If you want to use for Classification inform Director.

January 6 thru March 24 will be Classification and open shooting.

Time: Relay sign up and firing begins at 4:00 PM.

Cost: $4.00 per evening. (Donations beyond this accepted)

Equipment: Any safe 22 cal. Pistol or Revolver capable of firing 5 rounds in 10 seconds.
Open sights or Red (Green) Dot sights are acceptable.

Awards: Awards will be given to each Classification 1st & 2nd. Most improved shooter will be recognized..

Shooting Ahead: Shooters will be allowed to shoot ahead if they are unable to attend every week also allowed make up shooting.

Classification will be set after 120 match rounds fired (30 rounds per match) and maintained until season finale. Club classes will be used for Classification.

Classification:

Shooting is open to non-Members with a demonstrated shooting ability and safe firearms handling.
Shooters will be responsible to leave Range in a clean and orderly manner.
