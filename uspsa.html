2024 USPSA SUMMER LEAGUE

Matches will be held on the following days throughout the summer.

Set up begins around 8:00 AM. Registration is at 09:30 AM. Shooting starts at 10:00 AM and goes until it's done (around 1:00 pm). Please show up early and stay late to help set up and tear down.

Calendar of Events

April 21 - League 1
May 5 - League 2
May 19 - League 3
June 2- League 4
June 30 - League 5
July 13 - SATURDAY NWGC Points Match $25
July 21 - League 6
August 11 - League 7
August 18 - League 8
August 31 - Classifier Match - SATURDAY $20 - Plus Awards

 

We shoot 5 stages, one of which is typically a classifier.

Recommended to bring 150 rounds minimum and enough magazines for 30 - 50 rounds of ammunition (recommend 3-5 magazines at minimum).

 

Match fee is $15.00, payable in cash at the match (see match dates for exceptions) $5 to shoot a second gun (when practical).

 

Matches and scores will be posted on the club's Practiscore page: practiscore.com/clubs/northwestern-gun-club

Current league standings can be found on the USPSA SCORES page on the NWGC website.
