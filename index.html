Welcome to the New Home of the Northwestern Gun Club!

 

Established in 1907 Northwestern Gun Club is the oldest gun club in Minnesota, and is the oldest NRA Gun Club in America.
 
 
News, Notes & Upcoming Events!
 

The regular NWGC monthly meetings at 7 PM on the first Tues. of every month are being held at the Cast Iron Restaurant.

5906 Old Miller Trunk in Pike Lake. 2 Blocks north of Highway 53 on the Solway Road-Just Past the Pike Lake Stoplights.
